Part I: Websites

 

1.                        Scope

This Part describes the processing of personal data in connection with the use of our website. For the processing of personal data in connection with our mobile applications and services, see Part II below.

 

2.        Collection and Processing of Personal Data

You may visit our website without registering or otherwise submitting personal data.

We only collect personal data in order to process a support query, which you can submit at http://www.fdg-entertainment.com/#contact . In this case, we collect your name, company, email address and your query. The legal basis for the processing is point (b) of Article 6 (1) GDPR.

We will not use your personal data for other purposes than processing your query. We will not transmit your personal data with third parties unless we are legally required to do so, for example to criminal prosecution authorities. Your data will not be processed outside Germany or the EU.

 

3.         Cookies and pseudonymous user profiles

“Cookies” are pieces of information that a web site transfers to an individual’s hard drive for record-keeping purposes. Cookies allow the web site to remember important information that will make your use of the site more convenient. Like most web sites, FDG uses cookies for a variety of purposes in order to improve your on-line experience. For example, we track the total number of visitors to our site on an anonymous aggregate basis.

We may also employ cookies so that we remember you when you return to the site and to help customize your FDGs web experience. We may associate personal information with a cookie file in those instances. Your user profile, however, will always be pseudonymous, that means a random number is used for the cookie. We will not associate other personal data (like your name, address etc.) with your user profile.

The legal basis for the processing is point (f) of Article 6 (1) GDPR. The legitimate interests that we pursue are providing a user-friendly website and optimising our services.

Use the options in your web browser if you do not wish to receive a cookie or if you wish to set your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie. Click on the “Help” section of your browser to learn how to change your cookie preferences. If you disable all cookies, you may not be able to take advantage of all the features of this web site.

 

4.                        Links to Other Sites

We may offer links to sites that are not operated by FDG. If you visit one of these linked sites, you should review their privacy and other policies. We are not responsible for the policies and practices of other companies, and any information you submit to those companies is subject to their privacy policies.

 

5.                        Analytics Software

 

Our website uses Google Analytics, a service provided by Google LLC 1600 Amphitheatre Parkway Mountain View, CA 94043, USA, to collect information about the use of our web site. Google Analytics uses cookies and collects information on users visiting the web site, on what pages users  access, etc. The information collected about your use of our web site will be transmitted to and processed by Google on servers in the United States. Google collects only the IP address assigned to you on the date you visit this site. Your IP address will be truncated within the area of the European Union/European Economic Area. Only in exceptional cases the whole IP address will be first transferred to a Google server in the USA and truncated there. The data collected by Google Analytics will not be associated with any other data held by Google.

Google will use this information in order to evaluate your use of the web site, to compile reports on website activities and to provide other services relating to our web site. The legal basis for the processing is point (f) of Article 6(1) GDPR. Our legitimate interests are to analyse our web site traffic to improve the user experience and to optimise the website in general. You can prevent the collection and use of data (cookies and IP address) by Google by downloading and installing the browser plug-in available at https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout?hl=en. Further information concerning data privacy can be found at https://policies.google.com/privacy. Alternatively, you can use the options in your web browser if you do not wish to receive a cookie or if you wish to set your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie. Click on the “Help” section of your browser to learn how to change your cookie preferences. If you disable all cookies, you may not be able to take advantage of all the features of this web site.
