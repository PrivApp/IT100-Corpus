Data We Process and How Do We Use Information We Collect From You

Depending on how you interact with us, some data is automatically processed if you visit our Sites, install and use our Apps, whilst other data is collected and processed if you submit it to us.

  • Data we process when you visit our Site:

    • When you visit our Sites, your device automatically sends technical information to us that we store in log files, including the following "Data generated from Site":

    • Date and time of the visit and the duration of use of the Site;
    • The IP, of your device as well as your internet service provider (ISP);
    • The referral/exit URL;
    • The visited subsites of the Site;
    • Clickstream data;
    • Cookies and similar technologies; and
    • Information about your device including type of device, browser type and version, operating system.

    We process Data generated from Site to allow you to use our services available on our Sites and to ensure that its functionality. We also process these data to perform analyses on the performance of the Site, to continuously improve the Site, correct errors, to prevent fraudulent use, to ensure IT security and operation of our systems. We further process this data in aggregated or pseudonymized form to analyze trends, administer the Sites, track users' movement on the Site, and to gather demographic information about our user as a whole to improve marketing including serving you personalized, interest-based ads as well as to enhance user experience on our Sites.

  • Data we process when you use our Apps

    • Perfect processes Personal Data when you install and register for one of our YouCam Apps. Some of this information will be processed automatically when installing our Apps on your device. The following data are deemed as "Apps Installation Data":

    • operating system
    • device hardware
    • IP address
    • App version
    • device location, including a unique identifier for your device,
    • Firebased Cloud ID (FCM ID), MAC address, Advertising ID,
    • mobile network carrier, device model, locale, device resolution, SR number (for app version) and CPU architecture.

    We also collect certain "Apps Registration Data” during installation of our Apps onto your device. Although name, gender, date of birth, email address and password are mandatory for registration, we may ask you to submit other information (which is entirely optional), including:

    • your personal description and photograph in your profile;
    • postal address, telephone number;
    • beauty profile information like skin type, skin tone, hair colour and hair type, nails, eye attributes;
    • information on skin diary including skin log, acne detection data of your photos taken analysis of skin conditions and skin diagnostic results (collectively known as "Skin Diagnostic Results").

    We use Apps Installation Data and Apps Registration Data to enable the provision of maintenance, archive, backup (including Cloud Album) or support services to you. We will also use this information only with your consent to provide push notifications with information about our products, features, services offered by Perfect; or share your Skin Diagnostic Results with our affiliates who will do so solely on our instructions and have undertaken to comply with strict contractual requirements for the security of your data (including, but not limited to, complying with this Privacy Statement).

    We collect information about the way you use our Apps after you have installed and registered them. The specific "User Apps Usage Data" varies depending upon which YouCam App you are using, but may include:

    • Launch data
    • Device data
    • Feature usage
    • Page clicks
    • Sign-in information
    • Facial Characteristics

    We process User Apps Usage Data so that we can facilitate, evaluate, and verify your use of our Apps and services as well as for our own internal statistical, archive, research and analytical purposes, and to evaluate and enhance users' experience of the Apps by identifying user preferences and analyzing crash data. For example, we process information like the time frame of your usages on Sites or Apps, search results and search history, type of post, videos and other content you viewed on our Sites/Apps as well as information about how you use features like our camera on our Sites and Apps.
    Specifically for the processing of facial characteristics information, we will only detect your facial feature vectors in order to apply real-time virtual try-on effects thereon, upon your usage of our apps. Neither your facial photo/video nor your facial feature vectors will be stored in our database in any form whatsoever, whether you are or aren't using our apps. However, if you spontaneously choose to opt-in our services or request for "get photo" via email, we will temporarily store your photos in our email server in order to send to you as instructed by you, provided that we will follow the guidelines of the Data Retention Period to keep the User Apps Usage Data.
