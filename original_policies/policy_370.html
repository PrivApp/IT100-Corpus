Cookies help us to manage the state of order and also present customize offers to our customers. When you access the game, we collect information in order to manage advertisements and provide a more personalized experience by analyzing your personal information. We use Log Files and tracking technologies to collect and analyze certain technical information. This information may include IP addresses, device type, browser language, platform type, the amount of time you spent on the game, date and time of activity, and similar information.

We may disclose your personal information. But don’t worry we will do it only on certain conditions. And these conditions are

When you play our games, we may collect general information about you, this information includes:
